# Vision

A trustworthy betting intelligence layer that:
- Covers global leagues and all major markets.
- Explains its picks (not black box): **probabilities, EV, color-coded confidence, GPT insights**.
- Publishes a transparent daily tipset (2–5 “Smart Bets”) with full historical outcomes and P/L.

**Long-term:** Live odds EV, API for partners, React/Vite app, Stripe subscriptions, mobile app, and community features.
